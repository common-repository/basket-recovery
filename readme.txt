Send automated reminder emails to users who abandon their shopping carts before making a purchase.

== Description ==

Increase your conversion rate by up to 20% with BasketRecovery.net

The plugin sends automated reminder emails to potential customers who abandon their shopping carts before making a purchase. Fully compatible with all Wordpress ecommerce systems including:

*   WooCommerce
*   WP E-Commerce Plugin
*   Quick Shop
*   Cart66
*   Jigoshop

It has the following features:

*   Customise the content and timing of up to five reminder emails
*   No visible changes to your site, and page load times are not affected
*   Plugin works as soon as email is entered - the signup form does not have to be submitted.
*   Monitor what emails have been sent, and their success
*   Ongoing customer support (24 hour email guarantee)
*   Tips on maximising conversion rate

The plugin comes with a 30 day free trial, and costs just $29.99 per month after that.

*** Until 30th June, BasketRecovery is half price ($14.99 for life) for all Wordpress users ***

For full details, and to create an account, go to www.basketrecovery.net/wordpress

== Installation ==


1. Upload `basket-recovery.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to www.basketrecovery.net/wordpress to create an account
